Top 5 Kannada Movies

  1. K.G.F: Chapter 1

    1. Year of Release: 2018
    2. Main Character: Rocky (played by Yash)
    3. Description: A period drama that traces the journey of Rocky, who rises from poverty to become the most powerful man in the gold mining fields of Kolar.

  2. Kirik Party

    1. Year of Release: 2016
    2. Main Character: Karna (played by Rakshit Shetty)
    3. Description: A coming-of-age film that follows the life of a group of engineering students and their journey through college life.

  3. Ugramm

    1. Year of Release: 2014
    2. Main Character: Agastya (played by Sriimurali)
    3. Description: A high-octane action thriller about Agastya, a reformed gangster who must confront his past to protect his loved ones.

  4. Mungaru Male

    1. Year of Release: 2006
    2. Main Character: Preetham (played by Ganesh)
    3. Description: A romantic drama that tells the story of Preetham, who falls in love with Nandini, but faces challenges as she is already engaged to another man.

  5. Lucia

    1. Year of Release: 2013
    2. Main Character: Nikki (played by Sathish Ninasam)
    3. Description: A psychological thriller that revolves around Nikki, an insomniac who comes across a drug called Lucia, which makes his dreams more realistic.
